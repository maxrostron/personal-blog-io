Just like an 'About' page tells you about someone, or a
'Contact' page informs you of how to get in touch, a 'Now' page tells you what a person is focused on at this point in their life.

<br />

This is a concept was invented by the brilliant thinker<a href="https://sive.rs/now" className="utility__sidenote-contents-link"> Derek Sivers</a>. He encourages people to briefly
write about the things you'd tell a friend you hadn't seen in a year to help
them catch up with you.
